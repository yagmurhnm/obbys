ABOUT

OBBYS is a successful start-up that provides the fashion world with a new and
more interactive design creation experience. OBBYS was created in 2022 by
five individuals. The founders of the firm had the ambition of bringing real-time
image processing technologies, computer vision, deep learning, and artificial
intelligence to the world of fashion.
